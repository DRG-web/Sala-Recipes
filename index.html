Chicken Marinade

A bright, simple marinade built on our house spice blend — olive oil and fresh lemon juice bring it all together.

Ingredients — per lb of chicken

  • 2 tbspOur house seasoning blend
  • 2 tbspOlive oil
  • 1 tbspFresh lemon juice
  • 1 lbBoneless chicken

Scaling up for a crowd? Multiply every ingredient by the number of pounds of chicken you're using — the ratio stays the same.

Method

  1. Whisk the marinade

    In a medium bowl, whisk together the seasoning, olive oil, and lemon juice until well combined and slightly emulsified.

  2. Coat the chicken

    Add the chicken and turn to coat thoroughly, making sure every piece is covered in the marinade.

  3. Marinate

    Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours. For the best flavor, marinate overnight.

    2 hrs minimum · overnight ideal

  4. Cook to your liking

    Grill, roast, or pan-sear the chicken until it reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).
